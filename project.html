Introduction

As you know, right now what is condition of stray dogs in your respective areas or residents, its bitter but truth, that the street dogs suffer alot than your expensive purchased furry friends, they have to roam every where for some little food for example like garbage bins, drains etc. these mentioned examples are the live examples, you all can this by just wathing them. Due to this condition, they have to suffer severe problems like one of the most dangerous is CPV (parvo). So this site is made with a purpose and the purpose of making this website is to make a good living for every street dogs. This is the platform where you can Request or Donate medicine for these dogs or you can adopt then and many more things. Misguided compassion towards street dogs often leads to major human-dog conflict and worsens the problem. Every action of individuals or animal welfare organisations must be carefully thought through, ensuring the welfare of both people and animals.


Our goal is to help stray dogs in need. We support local street rescuers who love and care for the dogs in their local community, but do not have the resources to do so. We provide food, medicine, shelter and emergency vet care.
